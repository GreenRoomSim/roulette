The Wheel

European-style single-zero wheel — 37 pockets (0–36). A single zero means a lower house edge than American double-zero wheels. Straight-up bets pay 35:1, and payouts scale down as you cover more numbers per bet.

How Borrowing Works

Broke? Visit the Banker to borrow chips. Whatever you borrow, you owe 150% back, rounded up — borrow 2, owe 3; borrow 10, owe 15. You can never carry more than 100 chips of debt at once. Pay it down any time straight from your chip balance on the Bank screen.
